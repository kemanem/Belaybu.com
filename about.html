About Belayneh Bahiru

Dedicated to serving our community with integrity, compassion, and unwavering commitment to progress.

My Story

Born and raised in Ethiopia, I have dedicated my life to public service and community development. My journey began with a deep understanding of the challenges facing our people and a commitment to creating positive change through collaborative leadership.

Throughout my career, I have worked tirelessly to bridge divides, bring people together, and advocate for policies that improve the lives of all citizens. My approach is rooted in listening, understanding, and taking action based on the real needs of our community.

I believe that true leadership comes from service, and that sustainable progress is only possible when we work together, respecting our diversity while focusing on our shared goals and values.
